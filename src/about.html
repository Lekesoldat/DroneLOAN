About

DroneLOAN was founded in 2015 by the norwegian entrepreneur Morten Rustad. Formerly, the company consisted of one tiny store close to the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU). However, as this is a quickly growing industry Morten has decided to move his industry online as well.

The main purpose of the web site is to ensure an user-friendly experience or both renting drones and learn about them. We all know that drones can be very expensive to purchase, and in such a growing industry where technology quickly becomes outdated, renting will be the best option.
